Background

Ziwei Long was born in Zhangye, Gansu, China. He graduated with a B.Eng. in Artificial Intelligence from Tongji University in Shanghai, China, in June 2024, having studied there from August 2020 to June 2024. From July 2024 to June 2025, he worked as a Research Assistant under Professor Rui Fan at the MIAS Group, Tongji University. Currently, he is seeking opportunities to pursue a PhD in the field of Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence.
